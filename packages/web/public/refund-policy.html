Refund Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

This Refund Policy explains how refunds work for purchases made on ReeeeecallStudy ("the Service"). ReeeeecallStudy sells digital products only. Web payments are processed either by our authorized reseller and Merchant of Record, Lemon Squeezy, or — for Korean-won (KRW) purchases — by TossPayments; in-app purchases on mobile are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play.

1. Subscriptions

Paid subscriptions (increased card-storage plans) are billed monthly and renew automatically until cancelled. You may cancel at any time; access continues until the end of the current paid period, after which no further charges are made.

If you are charged for a subscription and have not made substantial use of the paid features, you may request a full refund within 14 days of that charge. Renewal charges are generally non-refundable once the new period has begun, but if a renewal was charged in error or unused, contact us and we will review it in good faith.

2. AI Credit Packs

AI credit packs are one-time purchases of a consumable balance used for AI-powered card generation. Because credits are consumed as you use them:

  • Unused credits: refundable within 14 days of purchase if none of the credits from that pack have been consumed.
  • Consumed credits: once credits have been used, the used portion is non-refundable, as the corresponding service has already been delivered.

3. How to Request a Refund

Email luke@rictax.kr with the email address on your account and your order or receipt number. We aim to respond within 5 business days. Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method through the processor that handled the purchase (Lemon Squeezy, TossPayments, the App Store, or Google Play).

4. Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy limits any refund or cancellation rights you may have under the consumer-protection laws of your country. Where the law grants you a right to a refund (for example, a statutory cooling-off period), that right applies in addition to this policy.

5. Contact

Questions about a payment or refund? Contact us at luke@rictax.kr.
